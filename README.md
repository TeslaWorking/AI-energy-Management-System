# AI-Based Energy Management System

## Project Description

The AI-Based Energy Management System is designed to optimize energy consumption by predicting future electricity demand and making intelligent energy management decisions. The system uses Machine Learning and Reinforcement Learning techniques to improve energy efficiency, reduce operational costs, and support sustainable energy management.

---

## Problem Statement

Industries often experience high electricity costs due to unpredictable energy demand, peak power consumption, and inefficient energy utilization. Traditional energy management systems lack accurate demand forecasting and intelligent decision-making, leading to energy wastage and increased operational expenses.

This project addresses these challenges by using **XGBoost** for electricity demand prediction and **Reinforcement Learning (RL)** for intelligent energy management and resource optimization.

---

## Objectives

- Predict future electricity demand.
- Optimize energy utilization.
- Reduce peak power consumption.
- Minimize operational energy costs.
- Improve overall energy efficiency.
- Support intelligent and sustainable energy management.
